PB&J

Do You Need Me to Say More??

Because I can! Seriously. It's a damn PB&J. It's not rocket science. Put it in your peanut butter hole and like it.

Pro-tip:

Wipe off your knife. Use a different one. Literally anything is preferrable to crossing the streams. Don't be lazy!

credit: 30 Rock

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps:

  1. Toast the bread (if you're feeling froggy). One slice per slot. Set the toaster lower; you can't untoast that shit.
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice with a butter knife.
  3. Wipe the knife off, if you're not a maniac.
  4. Spread jelly on the other slice.
  5. Smoosh it together.
  6. Eat it!!!!!!!!

If it looks like this, then you've gone too far.
